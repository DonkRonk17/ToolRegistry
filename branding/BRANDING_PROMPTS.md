## Design Notes

**Tool Concept:** 
ToolRegistry is a unified discovery and management system for Team Brain's 35+ tools. It catalogs all tools, extracts metadata, provides search, and enables launching tools directly.

**Visual Metaphors:**
- Grid/Catalog: Represents organized registry of tools
- Highlighted cells: Discovery, search, finding the right tool
- Connected nodes: Ecosystem unity, tools working together
- Command center: Control, visibility, management

**Color Psychology:**
- Cyan: Precision, technology, clarity, discovery
- Dark navy: Professionalism, depth, reliability
- White accents: Clarity, organization, data

**Target Aesthetic:**
Organized command center - the place where you go to find and manage all your tools. Should feel like a control room or database interface, but elegant and modern.

**Consistency with Ecosystem:**
Follow Beacon HQ Visual System v1 for consistency with other Team Brain tools like SynapseLink, AgentHealth, etc.
